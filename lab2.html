What my day looks like:
  1. Weekdays

    2. I wake up at 6 am, eat breakfast, shower and get ready to commute to Humber. During my commute, I read my book,Atomic Habits, and the news and reach college around 8:30Pm I drink my coffee from Starbucks and attend classes till 3 Pm after which I head home.
    I often eat my lunch in the bus while travelling back and rest for until 6pm. Then I get fresh and start studying a little until its my time to gym at 7:30Pm.
    Afterwards, I shower and get dinner till around 10Pm and get into bed. Now, depending on my work, I either work and study at night or watch a movie and sleep by 12:30am

  2. Weekends

    3. I wake up at 9 am eat breakfast, shower and get ready. I read my book,Atomic Habits, and the news till around 12Pm. Then I head out to cute Cafes and often eat my lunch work there simultaneously.
    I try to get in 2 hours of studying done and take a little break by 5 for an hour. Then I try to catch up on my assignments until 7 pm and finally gym finish eating dinner by 9:30Pm and then go out with friends for the night.
